## What is iMocha?

iMocha is an AI-powered skills intelligence and talent assessment platform that helps enterprises make data-driven hiring and workforce development decisions. iMocha serves 300+ customers globally, including 15 Fortune 500 companies, across industries including technology, financial services, staffing, manufacturing, and professional services.

iMocha has been recognized with the following industry awards:
- Brandon Hall Gold Award for excellence in talent assessment technology
- SAP Top 10 Partner recognition
- Workday Silver Partner certification

iMocha's platform is built on two core product lines:
1. Talent Acquisition (TA) — pre-hire assessment, AI interviews, candidate screening
2. Skills Intelligence (SI) — employee competency mapping, skill gap analysis, upskilling, workforce development

---

## Talent Acquisition (TA) Product Line

The TA product line covers the full pre-hire assessment lifecycle. It is designed for hiring teams, recruiters, pre-sales engineers, and talent acquisition leads.

### Core TA Use Cases
- Pre-hire technical screening: assess candidates before interview with role-specific tests
- AI-powered conversational interviews: automated first-round interviews via Tara AI
- ATS-integrated assessment workflows: trigger assessments directly from the applicant tracking system
- Candidate ranking and shortlisting: score-based ranking for efficient recruiter review
- Diversity and inclusion analytics: funnel-stage demographic reporting and bias detection

### Tara AI — Conversational AI Interview Platform
Tara is iMocha's AI-powered interviewer. Tara conducts structured conversational interviews autonomously, without requiring a human interviewer on the call.

Key Tara AI specifications:
- Powered by Azure OpenAI GPT-4o
- Interview duration: 5–10 minutes per candidate
- Accuracy: 90% alignment with human interviewer scoring (validated against structured human interview panels)
- Technology: Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Human-in-the-Loop review, few-shot learning, SME validation
- Outcome: structured interview transcript, candidate score, recommendation, and flag for human review when confidence is low

Tara AI is designed for high-volume screening where scheduling a human interviewer for every candidate is not feasible. It does not replace final-round human interviews.

### TA Analytics
- Real-time recruiter dashboards: pipeline volume, time-to-fill, assessment completion rates
- Hire quality tracking: correlates assessment scores with post-hire performance data
- Assessment effectiveness reports: item-level analytics, pass rate trends, score distribution
- EEOC / UGESP compliance: demographic reporting aligned with EEOC Uniform Guidelines on Employee Selection Procedures (UGESP)
- Diversity funnel analytics: tracks candidate demographic breakdown at each stage

## Skills Intelligence (SI) Product Line

The SI product line is designed for HR, L&D, and workforce planning teams. It addresses employee development after hire — not pre-hire screening.

### Core SI Use Cases
- Competency framework management: define and manage role-based competency profiles
- Employee skill gap analysis: identify gaps between current employee skills and target role requirements
- Upskilling and learning path recommendations: surface relevant learning content based on gap data
- HRIS integration: sync employee data with UKG and other HRIS platforms
- LMS integration: connect with learning management systems to ingest completion data
- Workforce planning dashboards: visualize bench strength, succession risk, and skills distribution

### SI and TA — Product Boundary
TA and SI are separate product modules. TA features (ATS integration, pre-hire screening, Tara AI) do not automatically apply to SI, and SI features (competency frameworks, internal mobility) do not apply to TA unless explicitly documented.

If a client requirement is unclear about whether it is pre-hire (TA) or post-hire (SI), clarify before mapping to a product.

## Deployment and Infrastructure

iMocha is delivered as a SaaS platform. The platform maintains a 99.9% uptime SLA.

Infrastructure highlights:
- Transport encryption: TLS 1.2+
- Data encryption at rest: AES-256
- Secrets management: Azure Key Vault
- Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliant (assessment player and admin portal)
- Security certifications: ISO 27001:2022, SOC 2 Type II
