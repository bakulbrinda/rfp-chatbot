## Skills Intelligence Overview

iMocha's Skills Intelligence (SI) product line helps enterprises map, measure, and develop the skills of their existing workforce. SI is a post-hire, employee-facing product — it is distinct from iMocha's Talent Acquisition (TA) product line, which covers pre-hire screening.

SI is used by HR, L&D, and workforce planning teams to:
- Build and manage competency frameworks for all roles
- Identify skill gaps between current employee capabilities and target role requirements
- Recommend and track upskilling interventions
- Integrate with HRIS and LMS platforms to maintain a continuously updated skills profile
- Produce workforce analytics and reporting for CHROs, HR BPs, and business leaders

## SI Functional Requirements

### Competency Framework Management
- Admins can define a competency framework: a structured library of skills and competencies organized by role, job family, and proficiency level
- Each competency is defined with a name, description, and proficiency scale (typically 1–5: Awareness, Basic, Proficient, Advanced, Expert)
- Competency frameworks can be created from scratch or imported from an existing organizational framework via CSV or template
- Role profiles are defined by mapping required competencies and minimum proficiency levels to each job title
- Changes to competency frameworks are versioned; historical assessments are not retroactively rescored when frameworks change

### Employee Skills Profiles
- Each employee has a Skills Profile that shows their assessed proficiency level per competency
- Skills evidence sources:
  - iMocha assessments (highest confidence — directly validated)
  - HRIS data (job title, tenure, certifications from employee records)
  - LMS completions (course and module completions mapped to competencies)
  - Manager endorsements (where performance management integration is configured)
- Skills profiles are continuously updated as new assessment results and LMS completions are ingested
- Employees can view their own Skills Profile; managers can view profiles for their direct reports

### Skill Gap Analysis
- Individual gap analysis: given an employee and a target role, the system identifies which competencies are missing and which are below the required proficiency level
- Team / department gap analysis: aggregate view across a team, department, or the full organization
- Gap heatmap: visualizes skill coverage and gaps across departments and competency areas
- Critical gap identification: flags roles or departments where skill coverage falls below a defined threshold (e.g., fewer than 2 employees at required proficiency for a critical competency)
- Succession risk: identifies single points of failure (only one employee qualified for a critical role)

### Upskilling and Learning Path Recommendations
- Based on an employee's gap analysis, the system recommends learning resources to close identified gaps
- Learning recommendations are drawn from connected LMS content; SI does not host learning content itself
- Admins can configure the mapping between competencies and specific courses or learning paths in the connected LMS
- Progress tracking: SI tracks course completions from the LMS and updates the employee's Skills Profile accordingly

### Assessment Campaigns
- Admins can launch skills assessment campaigns targeting specific departments, role groups, or individuals
- Campaign management: set start/end dates, send reminders for incomplete assessments, monitor completion rates
- Assessment triggers: assessments can be scheduled periodically (e.g., annual recertification) or triggered by role change events from the HRIS

## SI Reporting and Analytics

### Employee-Level Reports
- Individual Skills Profile report: full view of an employee's competencies, proficiency levels, and evidence sources
- Skills gap report: shows gaps between employee's current profile and their current or target role requirements
- Learning progress report: tracks completion of recommended learning paths

### Team / Department Reports
- Department skills inventory: aggregate view of all competencies held by a department, with coverage percentages
- Skill gap summary: number and severity of gaps per competency across the department
- Upskilling progress: tracks completion rates and gap closure over time for a defined group

### Workforce Planning Reports
- Bench strength report: for each critical role, shows how many employees are ready now (at required proficiency), in progress (below proficiency but on a learning path), or at risk (no coverage)
- Succession planning: identifies employees who are ready or near-ready to fill senior or critical roles
- Scenario modeling: project the impact of role changes, headcount growth, or attrition on skill coverage

### Analytics Dashboard (Admin)
- Org-wide skills coverage: percentage of roles that have full competency coverage vs partial vs no coverage
- Assessment participation: completion rates by department, manager, and time period
- Skill trends: identifies skills improving or declining across the workforce over time
- ROI indicators: tracks upskilling investment against measurable gap closure

### Reporting Formats
- In-platform dashboards: real-time, filterable by department, role, competency, and time period
- Export: reports exportable to PDF and CSV for offline use or submission to leadership
- Power BI integration: SI data can be published to Power BI for custom enterprise reporting and visualization

## SI Integration Architecture

### HRIS Integration
SI integrates with HRIS platforms to ingest employee directory data and sync skills back to employee records.

Supported HRIS for SI:
- UKG (UKG Pro): bidirectional integration — employee roster inbound, validated skills outbound to UKG employee record
- Other HRIS platforms may be supported via REST API; confirm specific system support with the iMocha implementation team before committing

HRIS data ingested by SI:
- Employee roster: name, employee ID, email, job title, department, manager, start date
- Job changes: role changes, promotions, transfers — trigger profile updates and re-assessment recommendations
- Terminations: terminated employees are deactivated in SI (data retained per retention policy, not deleted)
- Certifications: certification records in the HRIS are ingested and mapped to relevant competencies

### LMS Integration
SI connects to Learning Management Systems to ingest course completion data and map completions to competencies.

LMS integration data flow:
- SI reads: course completions, module scores, learning path enrollment status
- SI does not write back to the LMS; integration is read-only from LMS
- Competency-to-course mapping is configured by the SI admin in the iMocha platform

### Power BI Integration
- SI publishes aggregated skills data to Power BI via the Power BI dataset API
- Custom dashboards and reports can be built in Power BI using SI data
- Data refresh frequency is configurable (default: daily)

## SI Policies and Governance

### Employee Data and Privacy
- Employees are notified before any skills assessment or profile-building activity
- Skills profiles are used for development and planning purposes — not for automated adverse employment decisions
- Employees can view their own profile and request corrections to inferred skills within 30 days
- Data retention: employee skills data is retained for the duration of employment plus 12 months (configurable); deleted on verified right-to-erasure request
