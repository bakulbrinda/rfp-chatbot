## Talent Acquisition Platform Overview

iMocha's Talent Acquisition (TA) platform covers the complete pre-hire assessment workflow: from job requisition to candidate screening, assessment delivery, AI interviews, scoring, and ATS score writeback.

The TA platform is used by hiring teams, recruiters, and pre-sales engineers to assess candidates for technical, functional, and behavioral roles before a human interview is conducted.

---

## TA Functional Requirements

### Assessment Delivery
- Assessments are delivered via a browser-based candidate portal — no plugin or software install required
- Candidates receive an assessment invitation link via email; link is time-limited and single-use
- Assessments support multiple question formats: multiple choice (MCQ), coding challenges, video responses, and scenario-based questions
- Proctoring: AI-powered proctoring monitors the candidate session for integrity; flags are reviewed by a human before any action is taken
- Accessibility: assessment player is WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliant; extended time accommodations are configurable per candidate

### Candidate Management
- Bulk candidate upload: recruiters can invite multiple candidates at once via CSV upload or ATS trigger
- Candidate status tracking: dashboard shows invitation sent, assessment started, assessment completed, results available
- Duplicate detection: system prevents re-inviting the same candidate to the same assessment within the active window
- GDPR / data deletion: candidate data can be deleted on request in compliance with right-to-erasure requirements

### Scoring and Reporting
- Auto-scoring: MCQ and coding questions are scored instantly on submission
- Score reports: each candidate receives a detailed report with section-level scores, time taken, and a pass/fail recommendation
- Recruiter dashboard: aggregate view of all candidates in a pipeline, sortable by score, completion status, and time
- Score writeback: completed assessment scores are written back to the ATS candidate record automatically (for supported integrations)
- EEOC / UGESP reporting: demographic reporting aligned with EEOC Uniform Guidelines on Employee Selection Procedures (UGESP) to support compliant selection processes

### Assessment Configuration
- Role-based assessment templates: pre-built assessment bundles for common roles
- Custom question authoring: recruiters and admins can add proprietary questions to any assessment
- Difficulty tiering: Entry-Level, Mid-Level, Senior, Lead/Architect difficulty tiers
- Time limits: configurable at assessment level or per question
- Pass/fail thresholds: set at section level or overall; configurable per assessment

## TA Analytics

### Recruiter-Facing Analytics
- Pipeline analytics: total candidates assessed, completion rate, average score, pass rate per assessment
- Time-to-fill tracking: measures time from assessment invitation to hire decision
- Score distribution: histogram of candidate scores across all invitees for a given assessment
- Comparative ranking: rank candidates against each other within the same requisition

### Admin-Facing Analytics
- Assessment effectiveness: item-level analytics — difficulty index, discrimination index, completion time
- Hire quality index: correlates assessment scores with 6-month and 12-month post-hire performance data (requires HRIS performance data integration)
- Diversity & inclusion funnel: tracks demographic breakdown at each stage of the hiring funnel; flags assessment questions with potential demographic bias
- EEOC compliance reports: exportable demographic reports aligned with UGESP requirements

## Conversational AI Interviews — Tara AI

### Overview
Tara AI is iMocha's AI-powered conversational interviewer. Tara conducts a structured first-round interview with a candidate autonomously — no human interviewer is required on the call.

Tara is designed for high-volume screening scenarios where it is not feasible to schedule a human interviewer for every applicant. It does not replace final-round or panel interviews.

### Technical Specifications
- AI model: Azure OpenAI GPT-4o
- Interview duration: 5–10 minutes per candidate
- Accuracy: 90% alignment with human interviewer scoring (validated against structured human interview benchmarks)
- Methodology: Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to ground responses in the role's job description and required competencies; Human-in-the-Loop review for low-confidence cases; few-shot learning from validated interview question-answer pairs; SME validation of scoring rubrics

### How Tara AI Works
1. Recruiter configures the interview: selects role, competencies to assess, and difficulty level
2. Candidate receives a link and joins the Tara AI interview session (browser-based, no install)
3. Tara asks structured questions aligned to the configured competencies; candidate responds by speaking or typing
4. Tara scores each response against a validated rubric
5. Interview transcript, score, and recommendation are available to the recruiter immediately after completion
6. For responses where Tara's confidence is below threshold, the session is flagged for human review before the recommendation is finalized

### Human-in-the-Loop Design
iMocha's Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) approach ensures no automated decision is made without a human review path:
- Low-confidence AI scores are automatically routed to a human reviewer queue
- No candidate is automatically rejected based solely on Tara AI's output — recruiters receive a recommendation, not a binary decision
- Recruiters can override any AI recommendation with a documented reason

### Ethical AI and Bias Mitigation
- Few-shot learning examples used to train scoring rubrics are reviewed by SME panels for demographic bias
- Scoring is competency-based, not language-style-based — regional accents and communication styles do not affect scoring
- iMocha's Ethical AI and AI Governance policies govern the design, training, and monitoring of Tara AI
- EEOC UGESP compliance: assessment questions and scoring are designed to avoid adverse impact; demographic impact monitoring is available in TA Analytics

## AI FAQ Responses

### Q: Does Tara AI work in languages other than English?
A: Tara AI currently conducts interviews in English. Multi-language support details are covered in the AI FAQ Responses document.

### Q: How does Tara AI handle a candidate who asks it a question?
A: Tara AI stays within the scope of the interview. It will acknowledge the question and explain that the session is structured for assessment purposes. It does not provide information about the role, company, or hiring process — those should be communicated separately by the recruiter.

### Q: Can the Tara AI interview be customized per role?
A: Yes. Recruiters configure the competencies, question depth, and pass threshold per job requisition. The same Tara AI engine is used, but the questions and rubric are tailored to the role.

### Q: What happens if a candidate's internet drops mid-interview?
A: The session state is preserved for 30 minutes. The candidate can reconnect and resume from where the interview was interrupted. If reconnection is not possible within 30 minutes, the recruiter is notified and can issue a new invitation.

### Q: Is Tara AI's scoring auditable?
A: Yes. Every Tara AI interview produces a full transcript, per-question scores, and reasoning notes. Recruiters and candidates (on request) can review the basis for every score. Audit logs are retained per iMocha's data retention policy.
